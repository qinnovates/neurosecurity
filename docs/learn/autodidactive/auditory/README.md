# Auditory Learning

Music that starts with story. A way to share where I come from and express what drives this work through sound.

[Listen on Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/artist/4gZIMNI8AKnuGoYbcOzZc3)

## Why Music

The research is technical. The frameworks are abstract. But the reasons behind them are deeply personal. Music lets me encode that personal layer — the experiences, the philosophy, the fire — into something people can feel without reading a whitepaper.

Every track starts from a real story. The knowledge is woven into the lyrics, but the entry point is human experience, not curriculum.

## How It Works

1. **Start with the story** — what happened, what it meant, what it taught
2. **Thread in the knowledge** — Stoic philosophy, neuroscience concepts, security thinking
3. **Write lyrics with symbolic layers** — surface meaning anyone can feel, hidden meaning for those who dig
4. **Produce and iterate** — generate audio, refine arrangement and vocal dynamics across versions
5. **Release and share** — the music carries the message further than papers can

## Sawdust (Album)

The debut project. Dark gospel blues rooted in Stoic philosophy and personal narrative.

### Tracks

| Track | Topic | Genre | Details |
|-------|-------|-------|---------|
| [Sawdust](stoicism/sawdust.md) | Stoicism, resilience, the dark room | Dark gospel blues / soul (Dm, ~74 BPM) | [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/artist/4gZIMNI8AKnuGoYbcOzZc3) |

**Sawdust** encodes 10 Stoic teachings from Marcus Aurelius into a call-and-response gospel blues track. Three layers of meaning: surface (building and breaking), emotional (love as the reason to build), and hidden (a personal story encoded in the symbolism). Full lyric breakdown and symbolic analysis in [sawdust.md](stoicism/sawdust.md).

### Production History

The track went through extensive iteration — 20+ versions across different approaches:

| Phase | Versions | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Part 1 (verse/chorus) | v4 through v12f | Vocal tone, arrangement, call-and-response dynamics |
| Duet | v1 through v3 | Male/female vocal balance, harmony structure |
| Full arrangement | v1 through v4 | Complete song structure, bridge, dynamics |
| Climax (final chorus) | v1 through v7 | Cathedral build, drum entry, role reversal |
| Gold rap (verse 2) | v1 through v2 | Rap delivery, energy shift, Kanye-style flow |

Each version has a paired JSON composition plan documenting the production decisions. The golden reference (`GOLDEN-REFERENCE-climax-v5`) captures the target sound for the final chorus cathedral build.

## Why This Works

- **Story-first** — personal narrative creates empathy before the lesson lands
- **Emotional encoding** — music triggers emotional memory pathways, improving recall
- **Dual coding** — information in multiple modalities is retained better
- **Passive exposure** — background listening builds familiarity with concepts
- **Spaced repetition** — songs you replay naturally create spaced review
