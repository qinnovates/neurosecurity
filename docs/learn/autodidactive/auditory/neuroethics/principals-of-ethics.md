# Principals of Ethics

**Full Title:** Principals of Ethics — Calling All Those Who Want to Help Provide Equity to Those Who Need It the Most

**Topic:** Neuroethics — Kevin's story, vision, and the principles he derived from a life of rebuilding
**Genre:** Cinematic neo-soul gospel
**Key:** Dm | **BPM:** 70
**Status:** Released

## Wordplay Structure

The title operates on three levels:
1. **Principals** = the teachers, leaders, mentors who shaped us
2. **Principles** = the ethical foundations we derived (consent, dignity, equity, accessibility, cognitive liberty)
3. **Principal** = primary, foundational — these are the principal concerns of our generation

The song never explains the wordplay. It just uses both meanings and lets the listener catch it.

## Why This Song Is Personal

This isn't a manifesto about neuroethics. It's the story of how one person arrived at neuroethics through lived experience:
- A grandmother who forgot her children's names but never forgot how to pray
- A body that stopped walking from B12 deficiency — rebuilt through neurogenesis
- 15 years building security firewalls at every company, then realizing the brain needs one too
- Three generations of rebuilding: grandfather's wood factory in bamboo forests, father cleaning floors chasing free land, Kevin's frameworks
- Standing at a window in New Jersey staring at the George Washington Bridge, the Freedom Tower, and the Statue of Liberty every day — three symbols of freedom visible from one place

## Symbolic Layer

### Three Levels of Meaning
1. **Surface:** A call for ethical technology and equity — build it right, build it for everyone. A call to action for anyone listening to take part.
2. **Emotional:** A man who rebuilt himself telling the world he wants to rebuild it too.
3. **Hidden:** The principals (teachers) who gave us principles (values) are the reason we became principal (primary) voices for those who have none. The song is a thank-you letter to everyone who handed him chalk when he had nothing.

### Symbolic Vocabulary
- **The cathedral** = the mind. Grandmother's cathedral crumbled from Alzheimer's. Someone else wants to build corridors in yours.
- **The table** = governance, the place where decisions are made. Also: the dinner table where grandmother couldn't remember names. Also: the workbench where grandfather shaped wood. "Engrained with the names" — double meaning: engraved AND in the grain of wood.
- **The chalkboard** = what principals write with. Temporary but formative. The lesson outlasts the medium. Chalk dust carries to the end.
- **The torch** = Lady Liberty's torch. The principles that guide us. A golden torch like thunder in the rain.
- **The bridge** = George Washington Bridge — crossing it carrying the torch for those who need to see. Physical landmark becomes metaphor for connection.
- **Consent** = the single syllable between healing and harm. The principle that locks from within.

### Symbolic Progression
**Cathedral (mind) → Chalkboard (ethics) → Torch (freedom) → Bridge (connection) → Table (justice)**
