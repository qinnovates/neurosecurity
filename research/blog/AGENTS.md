# blogs/ -- Astro Content Collection (Blog Posts + Field Journals)

## Structure
- `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md` -- Blog posts (technical/security articles)
- `YYYY-MM-DD-field-journal-NNN-slug.md` -- Field journal entries (raw research observations)
- `YYYY-MM-DD-changelog-summary.md` -- Auto-generated changelog posts
- `README.md` -- Frontmatter schema, pipeline docs, writing guide

## Conventions
- All posts use Astro content collection frontmatter (see README.md for schema)
- Posts with `type: case-study` + `case-study` tag route to `/research/papers/[slug]/`
- Field journal entries preserve Kevin's voice and observations
- Fact-check gate applies to all posts before publication
- Changelog summaries are auto-generated by `npm run changelog` -- do not edit manually
- All neural/BCI claims must pass the 6 neuromodesty checks (see `rules/epistemic-integrity.md`)
- DSM-5-TR references require "for threat modeling purposes" qualifier
